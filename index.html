Kerala Startup Commons



About

Growth and success of startups in Kerala is our first priority. Kerala Startup Mission in keeping with the vision of the state government regarding encouraging startups in various sectors would like to plan to set up Kerala Startup Commons which shall provide all vital information about the basic requirements to start a company and help startups to move forward. Kerala Startup Commons is an effort by Kerala Startup Mission to onboard a collective of agencies and individuals who are willing to support startups during its various phases of growth right from inception to IPO.
